#  Executive Summary
Organizations often experience declining profitability despite steady revenue growth due to hidden inefficiencies known as **revenue leakage**.  
This project simulates a **Big-4 consulting engagement**, identifying revenue leakage drivers and evaluating true profitability across products, regions, and customers using data-driven analysis.


# Business Objectives
- Identify and quantify **revenue leakage sources**
- Measure **true profitability beyond topline revenue**
- Analyze margins across **products, regions, and customers**
- Enable **data-driven cost optimization and pricing decisions**
- Deliver **executive-level insights for leadership**

# Problem Statement
Despite stable sales volumes, the organization is facing **declining net margins**.  
Management lacks visibility into how discounts, returns, and operational costs impact profitability and which business segments are eroding profit.


# Key Business Questions
- Which products generate high revenue but low or negative profit?
- What percentage of revenue is lost due to discounts and returns?
- Which regions and customers contribute most to margin erosion?
- What are the top drivers causing revenue leakage?
- How can profitability be improved without increasing sales volume?
